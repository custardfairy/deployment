High Femme in Film

Madeline Kahn, Clue

Madeline Kahn was iconic in the role of merry widow Mrs. White, having seen five husbands dispatched in short order. The "Flames" scene is probably the most memorable from the film, with Madeline portraying femme rage with alacrity.

Meryl Streep, The Devil Wears Prada

Miranda Priestly was the nightmare boss in high fashion film The Devil Wears Prada. Though working for Miranda would be hell, this femme brings the attitude, style, and command of an icon.

Greta, Gremlins 2

Mean, green, and killing it in leopard print, Greta is the sole Femme mogwai. She is a gremlin, a creature of the night, and a monster. Who can't relate?

Here Comes the Bride

Anjelica Huston, The Addams Family

No one brought the goth femme aesthetic to the screen more than Anjelica Huston as Morticia Addams. From long black tresses and red nails to her elegantly tailored gowns, this femme is a dark romance role model.
